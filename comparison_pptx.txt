Strengths and Weaknesses of AI
1. What do we know about the current state of AI and machine learning?  What major successes can you remember, from either news stories or the first lecture?

3. Can we speculate as to why AI/ML tends to be good at some things, but not others?

[Slide 5]
The Elephant in the Room, ChatGPT (or similar)
1. What have you used ChatGPT, GPT-4, or another large language model for?
2. What have you heard about how ChatGPT works?
3. What are your opinions on how it ought to be used by students, versus misused?
4. Is this a case of AI researchers unleashing a technology on the world without adequately thinking of the consequences, or does it appear to be a net good?

[Slide 6]
Fears and Ethics
1. What do you worry *might* happen in AI and machine learning? 
2. What can AI/ML people do to help ensure that none of that comes about?

Making It Personal

What do you, personally, hope to get out of this class?
If you got *really* good at AI/ML eventually, what would you do then?
